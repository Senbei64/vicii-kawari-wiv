Char to show to relationships between dot4x and col16x clocks. Use to understand the timing in addressgen.v
16 ticks of the dot4x clock represents one half phi cycle.

For NTSC, for every 16 ticks of the dot4x clock, we get 28 ticks of the col16x clock.
For PAL, for every 16 ticks fo the dot4x clock, we get 36 ticks of the col16x clock.
